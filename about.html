About Us

We Empower Communities through Sustainable Agriculture & Conservation

Mundu Agricultural Environmental and Educational Organization (MAEEO), established in 2003, is a non-profit NGO based in Machakos, Kenya. Focused on the marginalized and drought-prone regions of Machakos, Makueni, and Kitui counties, MAEEO works directly with grassroots community groups, particularly women and youth, to implement sustainable projects in ecological agriculture, environmental conservation, and community empowerment.

Legal Status of NGO

Our organization is officially registered with the NGO Co-ordination Board under the following registration details:

Our Vision

MAEEO envisions empowered communities capable of transforming their lives, economies, and environments.

Our Mission

Our mission is to alleviate poverty through sustainable, community-driven development that respects and preserves the environment.

Collaborations and Achievements

MAEEO has worked with notable organizations such as DANIDA, the European Union, the ILO, and the Green Belt Movement. These partnerships have led to key initiatives in:

  • Environmental conservation
  • Agricultural development
  • HIV/AIDS awareness and gender equality

A highlight is the World Bank-funded project in Makueni County, where MAEEO supported over 3,000 farmers in improving their yields and livelihoods.
